The sites, tools and communities this handbook draws on and points to. The living sources are the
official modding wiki and the community Discord; PalCodex curates and organizes on top of them.

## Communities & primary docs

- **[Palworld Modding Wiki](https://pwmodding.wiki/docs/intro)** - the primary community documentation
  and the canonical step-by-step for many techniques curated here.
- **[Palworld Modding Community Discord](https://discord.gg/qHTZNcvYsv)** - where this knowledge is
  shared and discussed; the source of the credited guides.
- **[Official Pocketpair Discord](https://discord.gg/pocketpair)** - the game's official community.
- **[Palworld on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/1623730/Palworld/)** - the game.

## Core tools

- **[FModel](https://fmodel.app/download)** - browse and export cooked assets. See
  [FModel](/tools-and-setup/fmodel/).
- **UAssetGUI** - edit cooked `.uasset`/DataTables. See [UAssetGUI](/tools-and-setup/uassetgui/).
- **repak / retoc** - pack and unpack containers. See
  [repak & retoc](/tools-and-setup/repak-and-retoc/).
- **[RE-UE4SS](https://github.com/UE4SS-RE/RE-UE4SS)** - the scripting runtime. See
  [UE4SS](/frameworks/ue4ss/).
- **[Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org/)** - audio editing for [audio mods](/audio/).

## Community guide repositories

- **[Reaper's Palworld_Modding_Guides](https://github.com/ReapersVigilance/Palworld_Modding_Guides)** -
  including the [Kawaii Physics](/asset-modding/kawaii-physics/) guide.
- **[UnrealModSkeletonFixer](https://github.com/ShiftyChesh/UnrealModSkeletonFixer)** - Shifty's tool for
  [custom animations](/animations/custom-animations/).
