Translation mods localize Palworld into other languages by editing its text data and packaging the
result.

## In this section

- **[Translating Palworld](/translations/translating-palworld/)** - the workflow for translating the
  game's text into another language.

Text often lives in DataTables and localization files, so the [Data modding](/data-modding/) and
[Tools & Setup](/tools-and-setup/) sections are useful companions. For a schema-driven approach to
adding translated strings, see [PalSchema](/frameworks/palschema/).
