Audio modding replaces the game's sounds and music - Pal cries, UI sounds, background tracks - by
swapping the audio assets and repacking them.

## In this section

- **[Audio replacement](/audio/audio-modding/)** - replacing game audio and repackaging it.

Like other asset work, audio mods are packaged with the [pak pipeline](/packaging/); the
[tools](/tools-and-setup/) section covers extracting the originals.
