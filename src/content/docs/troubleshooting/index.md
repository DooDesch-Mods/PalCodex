The problems that come up again and again in the help channels, collected with their fixes so you don't
have to scroll Discord to find them.

## In this section

- **[FAQ](/troubleshooting/faq/)** - the questions asked most often.
- **[Common errors](/troubleshooting/common-errors/)** - crashes and load failures, and what causes
  them (mismatched builds, double-loading, bad packaging).
- **[Compatibility & updates](/troubleshooting/compatibility-and-updates/)** - what breaks after a
  game patch and how to get back to a working state.

If your problem isn't here, the [Palworld 1.0 vs early access](/getting-started/modding-1-0-vs-early-access/)
page explains the single most common root cause: following a guide written for a different game build.
